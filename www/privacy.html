Privacy Policy

Itikaf exists so you can speak freely about what's on your heart. That only works if you can trust what happens to your words. Here's exactly what we do and don't do.

What we never do

  • We never sell, rent, or share your data with data brokers, advertisers, or any third party for marketing purposes — no exceptions.
  • We do not use ad SDKs, analytics SDKs, or location-tracking SDKs of any kind.
  • We do not retain your raw audio recording. It is sent for transcription and deleted immediately afterward — it is never written to storage.

What we do

  • Your spoken entry is transcribed and briefly processed by our AI to find a relevant dua, verse, or sunnah practice for you. That processing happens in memory only and is not logged.
  • Once processed, your journal text is encrypted on your own device before it is ever stored, using a key that stays on your device. We cannot read your stored journal entries.
  • If your entry suggests you may be in crisis, we show you crisis resources (such as the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) instead of, or alongside, a normal reflection. Itikaf is not a substitute for a qualified scholar or mental health professional.

Your control

  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings.
  • Because encryption keys live on your device, uninstalling the app without exporting your key means stored history becomes unreadable, including to us.
